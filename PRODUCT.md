# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
High-end consumers and gourmet home chefs looking for premium meat cuts (steaks, BBQ, custom cuts, fresh and frozen meats) online who value premium quality, trust, and ease of ordering, as well as store administrators managing inventory, products, and order fulfillment.

## Product Purpose
To offer a luxury digital butcher shop storefront for "El Talkhawy" that showcases premium meats, facilitates a smooth localized ordering experience (Arabic/English with RTL support), and represents the craftsmanship of high-end butchery.

## Brand Personality
- Luxurious & Upscale: An artisan culinary experience with a warm, dark steakhouse vibe (charcoal, gold, burgundy).
- Passionate & Craft-driven: Celebrating butchery as an art, emphasizing precision, cuts, and culinary expertise.
- Trusted & Premium: Underpinning visual richness with clean layouts, strict hygiene imagery, and premium presentation.

## Anti-references
- Flat, low-contrast SaaS grays and generic mockups.
- Overly rounded "bubble" interfaces (e.g., card border-radius > 16px, button border-radius > 8px).
- Sketchy doodles or generic placeholder illustrations.
- Over-cluttered supermarket sites with neon banners.

## Design Principles
- Culinary Theater: Use high-quality photography, structured menus, and premium typography to make products look exceptionally appetizing.
- Clean Elegance: Maintain generous spacing, clear contrast, and premium type sizing to evoke high-end restaurant menus.
- Bilingual Craft: Provide equally premium visual treatment for both English and Arabic, respecting RTL grids and typography rules (Cairo + Libre Baskerville).
- Restricted Palette: Keep colors committed to the rich steakhouse palette (charcoal, burgundy, gold) rather than generic web grays.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios of at least 4.5:1 for body copy and 3:1 for large text.
- Full screen-reader semantic HTML support and visible keyboard focus states.
- Respect reduced-motion preferences with smooth transitions and optional static fallbacks.
